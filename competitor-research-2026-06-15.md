## Your Business Summary

**FactoryLM** (factorylm.com) — AI-powered maintenance digital transformation platform.
- Product: **MIRA** (Maintenance Intelligence Resource Agent)
- Core value: Structures messy maintenance reality (manuals, PLC tags, tribal knowledge) into a Maintenance Intelligence Namespace, then runs AI on top
- 68,000+ chunks of OEM documentation indexed (Allen-Bradley, Siemens, ABB, Schneider, Yaskawa, Mitsubishi, Rockwell, Honeywell)
- Pricing: $500 assessment → $2–5K/mo pilot → $499/mo operating layer
- ICP: Plant maintenance teams in industrial manufacturing

**CraneSync** (cranesync.com) — AI-powered field service assistant and real-time monitoring platform for industrial equipment maintenance, starting with overhead cranes. Includes a two-part system: crane condition monitoring + AI-driven service management platform for service managers.

**Combined space:** Industrial AI maintenance intelligence, CMMS, field service management for overhead cranes, predictive maintenance, IIoT, OT/IT integration.

## Key Themes & Opportunity Signals

**1. The "Structure First, AI Second" thesis is winning** — Cognite, Sight Machine, FactoryLM, and Palantir AIP all converge on the same architecture. FactoryLM's $500 assessment + namespace-builder approach is the most accessible entry point in this category.

**2. Crane/field service is underserved** — crewOS is the only direct overhead crane FSM competitor found. CraneSync's AI-powered monitoring angle has very few direct rivals at the AI layer for cranes specifically.

**3. CMMS is commoditizing** — Limble, MaintainX, UpKeep are all racing to add AI on top of work order management. FactoryLM's advantage is starting from the intelligence layer and adding CMMS, not the reverse.

**4. Industrial AI funding is strong** — Tractian ($200M total), Augury ($75M + $1B valuation), IndustrialMind ($1.2M seed in weeks). This is a hot VC category; FactoryLM's bootstrapped $499/mo model is a moat against VC-burned competitors.

**5. Enterprise incumbents are waking up** — Rockwell, Emerson (AspenTech), PTC are all adding AI to existing platforms. The window for FactoryLM to own the SMB/mid-market namespace-builder category is now.
