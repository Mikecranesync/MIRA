# Competitive Landscape: FactoryLM / MIRA & CraneSync
**Generated:** 2026-06-04 | **Scope:** Industrial Maintenance AI, Predictive Maintenance, CMMS, Connected Worker, Crane Technology

---

## Your Business Summary

**FactoryLM / MIRA** (factorylm.com) — AI-powered industrial maintenance platform that structures "messy maintenance reality" (manuals in filing cabinets, mismatched PLC tags, tribal knowledge) into a Maintenance Intelligence Namespace, then runs MIRA — an AI diagnostic assistant — on top. Key offerings: $500 floor assessment, $2–5K/mo pilot, $499/mo operating layer. Covers OEM documentation from Allen-Bradley, Siemens, ABB, Schneider, Yaskawa, Mitsubishi, Rockwell, Honeywell. 68,000+ documentation chunks indexed.

**CraneSync** (cranesync.com) — AI-powered field service assistant and real-time monitoring platform for industrial equipment maintenance, starting with overhead cranes.

## Key Takeaways

**FactoryLM / MIRA's sharpest competitors:** RepairXpertAI (#4), Energent.ai (#24), Factory AI (#21), and MaintainX (#3) are most directly in your lane — AI-grounded maintenance assistance for industrial plants, with varying OT/namespace depth.

**CraneSync's sharpest competitors:** Konecranes (#12) is the 800-lb gorilla (OEM-owned data, 30K connected units). The opportunity is OEM-agnostic AI field service and real-time monitoring that works across any crane brand.

**Your defensible moat:** UNS namespace structuring, PLC tag reconciliation, manual indexing with citations, and grounded (non-hallucinating) AI responses tied to a customer's actual plant. No funded competitor is doing all four together at the SMB/mid-market price point.
