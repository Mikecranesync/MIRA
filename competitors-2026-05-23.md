# Industrial AI Maintenance — 25 Competitors & Innovators
**Generated:** 2026-05-23 | **For:** FactoryLM / MIRA / CraneSync

## Business Context

**FactoryLM / MIRA** turns unstructured maintenance reality (manuals in filing cabinets, mismatched PLC tags, tribal knowledge) into a structured Maintenance Intelligence Namespace, then MIRA delivers grounded AI diagnostics on top. Targeting plant maintenance technicians via Slack, Telegram, and web.

**CraneSync** is an AI-powered field service assistant and real-time monitoring platform for industrial equipment, starting with overhead cranes — a vertical-specific extension of the same thesis.

### 1. Tractian
**URL:** https://tractian.com
**Category:** AI-driven predictive maintenance + CMMS (hardware + software)
**What they do:** Combines proprietary IoT vibration/temperature sensors, machine learning, and a built-in CMMS. Handles condition monitoring, asset performance management, and auto-generated work orders. Targeting brownfield manufacturers.
**Funding / Scale:** $196M raised across 5 rounds (Series C: $120M led by Sapphire Ventures). Backed by General Catalyst, Next47, NGP Capital.
**Why relevant:** Direct competitor — full-stack AI maintenance with CMMS. Their sensors + ML approach is hardware-dependent vs. MIRA's software-first namespace approach.

## MIRA's Differentiation (vs. all 25)

The competitive moat holding against all of these:

1. **Namespace-first architecture** — MIRA grounds every answer in the customer's actual Unified Namespace (ISA-95 ltree). No competitor below enterprise tier does this.
2. **68,000+ OEM documentation chunks indexed** — pre-built knowledge for Allen-Bradley, Siemens, ABB, Schneider, Yaskawa, Mitsubishi, Rockwell, Honeywell.
3. **PLC tag-to-asset reconciliation** — bridges the IT/OT gap that CMMS vendors ignore.
4. **$500 assessment entry point** — lower friction than any hardware-first or enterprise competitor.
5. **CraneSync vertical focus** — specialized AI diagnostics for overhead cranes creates a defensible niche that broad platforms won't prioritize.
