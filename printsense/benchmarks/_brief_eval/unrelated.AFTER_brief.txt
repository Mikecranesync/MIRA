🩺 If you're chasing a fault
If an input LED (say one of IN 0–11) does not light when the field device operates, isolate device-versus-wiring at two points: (1) at the sensor/field-device end, confirm the device actually switches its output; (2) at the corresponding green input terminal on the Micro820, confirm that same switched signal arrives. If the device switches but nothing arrives at the PLC input terminal, the fault is the wire/terminal between them; if the signal is present at the PLC terminal but the IN LED still does not turn on, the fault is in the input point/module.
