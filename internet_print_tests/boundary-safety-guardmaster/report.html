# Figure 27 — SafeZone Mini Laser Scanner, E-stop, and DI Safety Relay with Kinetix 350 Drive

This wiring diagram shows how a **SafeZone Mini laser scanner** (catalog **442L-SFZNMN**, cable **442L-ACABL210**), an **E-stop** (**800FM-MT44** / **800F-MX02S**), and a **DI safety relay** (**440R-D22R2**) are interconnected to provide dual-channel safety stop inputs to a **Kinetix 350 drive**, with status fed back to a PLC.

## Power
- **+24V DC** rail feeds: SafeZone Mini Brown wire, DI relay **A1**, Kinetix 350 terminal **1 (+24V DC)**, and PLC input modules.
- **24V DC Com** rail returns: SafeZone Mini Blue wire, DI relay **A2**, Kinetix 350 terminal **2 (Com)**, and PLC commons.

## SafeZone Mini wiring
- **Brown** → +24V DC
- **Blue** → 24V DC Com
- **Shield** → chassis ground
- **White (Warning Field)** → drives the **Warning Field** lamp
- **Pink** and **Gray** → dual-channel OSSDs into DI relay terminals **S12** and **S22**

## E-stop wiring
The 800FM-MT44 / 800F-MX02S E-stop contacts land on DI relay terminals **S11** and **S21** (the two safety input channels).

## DI Safety Relay (440R-D22R2)
- **Inputs:** S11/S21 from E-stop; S12/S22 from SafeZone Mini OSSDs.
- **Monitoring loop:** **S34** ties back to **S32** (EDM/loopback).
- **Reset:** momentary **Reset** pushbutton wired between **S34** and **Y32** (with L11/L12 in the reset circuit).
- **Logic selector:** rotary switch on the front of the relay (positions 0–8) — set per application.
- **Safety outputs:** contact pairs **13/14** and **23/24** go to the Kinetix 350 safety inputs.

## Kinetix 350 Drive connections
- **L1, L2, L3** — three-phase power input.
- **1** +24V DC, **2** Com — control power.
- **3 Status** — status output to PLC input module.
- **4 Safety Input 1** — fed from DI relay contact 13/14.
- **5 Safety Common** — common for safety inputs.
- **6 Safety Input 2** — fed from DI relay contact 23/24.
- Motor **M** on the drive output.

## PLC I/O shown (catalog options)
- **Inputs:** 1756-IB16, 1769-IQ16, 1746-IB16, 1734-IB4, 1793-IB6 (and 1756-IV16, 1769-IV16, 1746-IV16, 1734-IV4, 1793-IV16 variants).
- **Outputs:** 1756-OB16, 1769-OB8, 1746-OB4, 1734-OB2, 1793-OB4.
- The PLC processor monitors the Kinetix 350 **Status** output and the DI relay state.

## How it works (function)
Either the E-stop or a SafeZone Mini field breach opens both safety channels into the DI relay, which drops out contacts 13/14 and 23/24, removing the safety enable at Kinetix 350 terminals 4 and 6 and stopping the drive. The Reset button restores operation only after both channels re-close and the EDM loop (S34–S32) is healthy.

---

**Note:** This print shows wiring only — it does not indicate present contact state. Verify any "is it closed/energized" question with a meter at the terminals listed above. The DI relay **LOGIC** switch position must be set per the 440R-D22R2 installation instructions (not specified on this sheet).

No DANGER / WARNING / CAUTION boxes, hard prohibitions, or required reset/isolation/bypass instructions are printed on this sheet, so no safety section is required.
