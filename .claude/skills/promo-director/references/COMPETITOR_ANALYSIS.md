## Cross-vendor patterns

### Winning hooks (2025–2026 vintage)

| Pattern | Example | Transfer to MIRA |
|---|---|---|
| Question hook: "What if [existing workflow] with [new capability]?" | UpKeep: "What if you could control all of your maintenance data using just your voice?" | "What if your tech already knew the fault before they reached the machine?" |
| "Doesn't just X — it Y" (passive→active dichotomy) | UpKeep: "doesn't just analyze…it acts on it" | "doesn't just surface the fault code — it tells you exactly what to do next" |
| "You assign. It does." compression | Notion: "You assign the tasks. Your Agents do the work." | "You take the call. MIRA has the answer." |
| ROI proof via named customer + specific number | Augury: Fortune Brands 2.5x ROI, 1,000 machines | Plant name + "from first alarm to root cause in [X mins]" |
| Category creator: "industry's first AI for [niche]" | Augury: first AI for 1–150 RPM | "first AI trained on OEM fault libraries for [equipment type]" |
| Educational top-of-funnel outperforms product demo | Inductive Automation: "What Is SCADA?" was most-watched | "What Is Predictive Maintenance?" or "How to Diagnose a Bearing Fault" |
| Proof-point stat: dramatic before/after ratio | Vercel: coding-agent deployments <3% → >50% in 6 months | "fault-diagnosis time: [X hours] → [Y minutes]" with real customer |
| Conference keynote as primary launch vehicle | Stripe Sessions, Figma Config, Vercel Ship, Plaid Effects | Potential MIRA launch event tied to a customer site visit or OEM partnership |
| "Industrial Context Graph" / grounded AI platform | Augury: Machine Health + AVEVA CONNECT + Gemini → "Industrial Context Graph" | "MIRA's context graph starts with your OEM manuals — no sensors required" |
| Agent-fleet framing vs. copilot framing | Cursor 3.7: cloud VMs, /babysit, Canvases; Notion Workers; Anthropic Dynamic Workflows | "One MIRA agent per asset — already trained on its manual, already watching its tags" |

### Anti-patterns (avoid)

- Corporate narration over stock footage of factories with no specific claim → forgettable (common among mid-market CMMS vendors)
- Feature list VO without a single proof number → no hook, no memory
- "AI-powered" as the lead without explaining what the AI specifically does → now invisible signal (every competitor uses it)
- Paywalled conference recordings (Replit vibecon) — content doesn't compound; public YouTube compounds indefinitely

- **UpKeep**: shifted from feature-showcase to autonomous-agent narrative (Sep 2025 Nova launch). Now competing on workflow replacement, not improvement.
- **MaintainX**: acquired by Autodesk (May 2026). No longer a standalone CMMS startup; now an operations platform with CAD/BIM/digital-twin data flywheel. OEM manual ingestion claim (Feb 2025 CoPilot) remains; Autodesk adds design-data integration.
- **Augury**: moved from technical-demo format → Bloomberg third-party credibility (2025) → full platform play with named cloud/SaaS partners and "Industrial Context Graph" terminology (May 2026). Narrative converging on MIRA's knowledge-graph framing.

- **Inductive Automation**: doubled down on educational content (SCADA 101) over product feature content in 2025.
