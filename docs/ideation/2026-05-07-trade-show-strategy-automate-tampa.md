## Automate 2026 — short answer

**Skip the booth.** Free expo pass only, and only if there's an integrator-channel reason to be in Chicago anyway.

| Why | Fact |
|---|---|
| Wrong room | ~85–90% hardware show. Zero CMMS competitor presence (UpKeep / MaintainX / Limble / Fiix / Tractian all skip Automate; Limble is at IMTS). |
| Wrong math | Booth all-in $14K floor / $25–35K typical. At $97/mo LTV $1,164–$2,328, break-even is 8–16 customers. Goal is 3. |
| Wrong timing | Show ends 2026-06-25 → 27 days before deadline. Manufacturing software median sales cycle 60–120 days. |
| The one "go" case | Free expo pass + 10–15 pre-booked LinkedIn meetings with small integrators who serve manufacturing-SMB monday.com customers. ~$300 budget cap. Flips to "definitely go" if a first-degree integrator relationship exists. |

## Tampa expos — short answer

**Tampa isn't a maintenance/reliability conference city.** The one ICP-fit Tampa show (FB-FM, 2026-04-28) already happened.

| Show | Status | Move |
|---|---|---|
| FB-FM (Florida Buildings Engineering & Facility Maintenance Show) | Already past, was the right one | Book FB-FM 2027 booth ($1,825) when registration opens Q4 2026 |
| BAMA (Bay Area Manufacturers Assn) | Ongoing monthly mixers, very high ICP fit | **Join.** ~$500–$1,500/yr + $20–$40/event. Sponsor a May or June mixer. Only Tampa play that could touch the July 19 deadline. |
| AIChE Phosphate Fertilizer Conference (St. Pete Beach, 2026-06-05/06) | 29 days away, high ICP fit but enterprise-scale buyers | Optional learning trip ~$300–$500 registration. Phone-call pre-flight to AIChE Central FL chair to verify plant-floor vs corporate attendance. |
| APWA, IUCX, SWAN, SOF Week, Build Expo | Past or wrong ICP | Skip |
