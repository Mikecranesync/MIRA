## Your Business Summary
**FactoryLM** turns messy maintenance reality (manuals in filing cabinets, mismatched PLC tags, tribal knowledge) into a structured Maintenance Intelligence Namespace, then MIRA (the AI) runs on top. Pricing: $500 assessment → $2–5K/mo pilot → $499/mo operating layer. Serves plant maintenance technicians via Slack, Telegram, and web. 68,000+ OEM documentation chunks indexed.

**CraneSync** is an AI-powered field service assistant and real-time monitoring platform for industrial cranes — targeting the $45B crane industry with condition monitoring + AI-driven service management workflows.
