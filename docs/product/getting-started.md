# Getting Started

From zero to your first MIRA diagnosis in under 10 minutes.

## 1. Sign up

Go to [factorylm.com](https://factorylm.com) and click **"Start my beta."**

You'll enter:
- Your email
- Your name
- Your plant / company name

MIRA creates your tenant in **pending** status and sends a welcome email. Over the next 7 days you'll receive short Loom walk-throughs showing MIRA's capabilities. At day 7, you'll be invited to activate ($97/month, cancel anytime).

## 2. Activate

Click the checkout link in the day-7 email. Stripe handles payment. On successful payment:

- Your tenant is promoted to **active**
- Your Atlas CMMS workspace is provisioned automatically
- Your knowledge base seed pack is loaded (common vendors and fault codes, ready to go)
- You receive an **activated** email with your login link

## 3. Open MIRA on your phone

Visit [app.factorylm.com](https://app.factorylm.com) on your phone's browser (Safari on iOS, Chrome on Android).

**Recommended: install as a PWA.**
- **iOS Safari:** Tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen"
- **Android Chrome:** Tap the three-dot menu → "Install app"

The installed PWA opens full-screen like a native app, and MIRA's home-screen icon puts it one tap away.

## 4. Start your first diagnosis

Three ways to begin:

### Option A — Ask a question directly
Open MIRA, type or tap the mic: *"My Yaskawa GS20 is faulting on F030."*

MIRA will ask a few clarifying questions and walk you through diagnosis.

### Option B — Upload a nameplate photo
Tap the camera / attachment button, snap a photo of the machine's nameplate. MIRA reads the vendor and model via vision and scopes the chat automatically.

### Option C — Scan a QR sticker (recommended for daily use)
If you've tagged your equipment with MIRA QR stickers (see [QR asset tagging](qr-system.md)), open your phone camera, point at the sticker, tap the link. MIRA opens pre-scoped to that asset — no typing required.

## 5. Upload your manuals (optional but highly recommended)

MIRA works out of the box with 100+ common vendors in the shared knowledge base. For the best results, upload your plant-specific manuals:

- From the MIRA sidebar: **Knowledge → Upload PDF**
- Drag-and-drop or select from your file picker
- Manuals are processed in the background (2–10 minutes for a typical 300-page PDF)
- Once processed, MIRA cites these manuals in every relevant answer

## 6. Connect your CMMS (optional)

If you use Atlas CMMS: nothing to do. Your Atlas workspace was provisioned at activation.

If you use MaintainX, Limble, or Fiix: see [CMMS integration](cmms-integration.md) for connection steps.

Once connected, MIRA can:
- List open work orders for any asset
- Create new work orders from a diagnostic
- Auto-close work orders when a fault is resolved
- Pull recent fault history into every diagnosis

## 7. Invite your team

From the admin area: **Users → Invite**. Enter teammate email addresses. They'll receive an invite link to your plant's MIRA workspace. Role-based permissions let you separate **technicians** (chat + photo upload) from **admins** (user management, KB curation, QR printing).
