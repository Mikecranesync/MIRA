## What goes here

- **Legal posture:** Terms of Service / EULA / Privacy — what they disclaim, what they claim over customer data, liability caps, AI carve-outs. These leak product model and risk appetite.
- **Pricing surface:** When public, link to archive snapshots.
- **Product claims:** Feature lists straight from their marketing, dated.
- **Contact surface:** Support email, complaints email, founder/exec names when disclosed.
- **Our angle:** Where MIRA's positioning beats, matches, or lags them — and the one-liner we'd use on a call against them.

## What does NOT go here

- Scraped customer lists, private data, anything obtained outside of public sources.
- Speculation without a citation — mark unknowns as "unknown" rather than guessing.

## Source hygiene

Every dossier cites where the claim came from (URL + date pulled). Re-pull on a cadence — these sources change.
