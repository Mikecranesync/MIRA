## Strategic Observations for FactoryLM / CraneSync

1. **The namespace problem is validated.** Edmund AI, Cognite, and Siemens all explicitly acknowledge that AI cannot help until maintenance data is structured. FactoryLM's structuring-first thesis is industry consensus, not a contrarian bet.

2. **Most competitors skip the structuring work.** MaintainX, Limble, Tractian, and Augury all assume the data is already organized. FactoryLM's assessment → pilot → operating layer model monetizes the gap they all ignore.

3. **OEM copilots (Siemens, Schneider) are not a threat to multi-brand plants.** Any plant running mixed equipment — Allen-Bradley VFDs next to Siemens PLCs next to ABB drives — can't use a single OEM's copilot. That's FactoryLM's entire ICP.

4. **Edmund AI is the most direct threat.** Same PLC + docs + AI troubleshooting thesis, freshly funded, expanding to US market. Watch closely.

5. **CraneSync has a clear white space.** Konecranes TRUCONNECT only covers Konecranes equipment. CrewOS is workflow only. viHOI is safety-camera only. No one owns AI-powered field service + diagnostics for mixed-brand crane fleets.

6. **The market is growing fast.** Predictive maintenance market: $13.65B in 2025, projected $97B by 2034 (24% CAGR). MaintainX just raised $150M. Tractian grew 5,548% in 3 years. Timing is excellent.
