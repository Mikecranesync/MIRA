# Competitor & Innovator Research — June 16, 2026

**Scope:** 25 competitors and innovators in industrial maintenance AI, CMMS, and overhead crane field service — the spaces FactoryLM/MIRA and CraneSync operate in.

**Your businesses at a glance:**
- **FactoryLM / MIRA** (`factorylm.com`) — Structures messy maintenance reality (manuals, PLC tags, tribal knowledge) into a Maintenance Intelligence Namespace, then runs the MIRA AI copilot on top. Sells to industrial plants via Assessment ($500), Pilot ($2–5K/mo), and Operating Layer ($499/mo).
- **CraneSync** (`cranesync.com`) — AI-powered field service assistant and real-time monitoring platform for overhead cranes. Combines crane condition monitoring with AI-driven service management workflow automation.

### 1. MaintainX
**URL:** https://www.getmaintainx.com  
**Space:** AI-powered CMMS / maintenance copilot  
**What they do:** Mobile-first CMMS with an AI Maintenance Copilot that summarizes asset history, suggests probable causes and repair steps, with anomaly detection, parts prediction, and early fault detection via natural language. Strong team-communication model.

## Key Takeaways for FactoryLM / MIRA

1. **Namespace structuring is the undefended moat.** Every CMMS player claims AI but none structures OEM manuals + PLC tags + UNS into a grounded namespace. This is MIRA's core wedge.
2. **Tractian and Augury are the best-funded threats** ($196M and $369M respectively) but their moat is hardware sensors — MIRA wins at customers who already have data and want to make it queryable.
3. **Fiix/Rockwell customers are natural leads** — they have Allen-Bradley PLCs, live in the Rockwell ecosystem, and need a CMMS-agnostic AI layer that Fiix can't provide.
4. **Factory AI (f7i.ai) and OxMaint are the closest startup competitors** to MIRA's motion — watch their fundraising and product releases.

## Key Takeaways for CraneSync

1. **Redlist and crewOS are direct competitors** with more market presence today. Differentiate on real-time AI monitoring (not just inspection checklists).
2. **Samsara fleet customers are warm leads** — they have IoT on mobile equipment but zero crane-specific AI.
3. **No well-funded, AI-native crane specialist exists yet** — the market is ready for CraneSync to define the category before Redlist or crewOS raises and adds AI monitoring.
