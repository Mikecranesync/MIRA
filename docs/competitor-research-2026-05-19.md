## Your Business Summary (from factorylm.com + cranesync.com)

**FactoryLM** structures messy industrial maintenance reality — filing-cabinet manuals, mismatched PLC tags, tribal knowledge — into a *Maintenance Intelligence Namespace* (UNS, ISA-95). **MIRA** (Maintenance Intelligence Resource Agent) runs on top as an AI diagnostic copilot, grounded in that structured data, answering fault questions with citations from manuals, work-order history, and PLC tags. Pricing: $500 Assessment → $2–5K/mo Pilot → $499/mo Operating Layer.

**CraneSync** extends this to AI-powered field service and real-time monitoring for industrial cranes (starting with overhead cranes).

**Competitive positioning:** FactoryLM wins on *structuring first* before AI, vs. competitors that bolt AI onto unstructured data. The UNS gate and grounded-only diagnostic responses are the differentiator.
