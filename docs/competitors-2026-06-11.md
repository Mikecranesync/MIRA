# MIRA / FactoryLM — Competitor & Innovator Intelligence Report
**Generated:** 2026-06-11  
**Context:** FactoryLM (factorylm.com) structures industrial maintenance data — manuals, PLC tags, asset hierarchies, tribal knowledge — into a Maintenance Intelligence Namespace, then runs MIRA (AI maintenance copilot) on top. CraneSync (cranesync.com) is a companion product: AI-powered field service assistant and condition monitoring for overhead cranes.

---

## About FactoryLM / MIRA

FactoryLM sells the *structuring* work (Assessment $500, Pilot $2–5K/mo, Operating Layer $499/mo) that competitors skip. MIRA is the AI copilot that runs on top of the structured namespace, grounding every answer in cited manuals, PLC tags, work-order history, and real asset context — not hallucination. The key differentiator: **generic AI fails on the floor without a structured namespace; FactoryLM builds that namespace first.**

CraneSync specifically targets overhead crane service managers with real-time condition monitoring + AI-driven workflow automation.

### 1. MaintainX
**Website:** https://www.getmaintainx.com  
**Category:** AI-powered CMMS / Mobile-first work execution  
**Funding/Scale:** Well-funded Series C+ startup; ~4.8 stars Capterra  
**What they do:** Called the "WhatsApp of maintenance" — mobile-first frontline communication for work orders, SOPs, and operator rounds. Their **MaintainX CoPilot** generates answers from OEM manuals and work-order history. Anomaly detection flags deviations before escalation.  
**Why they matter:** Most direct product analog in the SMB/mid-market. Their OEM manual chatbot overlaps directly with MIRA's grounded Q&A. Strong mobile UX gives them frontline adoption others can't match.  
**Gap vs. MIRA:** No UNS structuring layer, no PLC tag reconciliation, no namespace-first approach. CoPilot answers are only as good as whatever manual was uploaded — no cross-asset knowledge graph.

### 11. Samotics
**Website:** https://www.samotics.com  
**Category:** Electrical Signature Analysis / Condition monitoring for harsh environments
