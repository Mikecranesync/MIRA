## Key Takeaways for FactoryLM

1. **PLC intelligence is a meaningful moat** — only Edmund AI, Metis, and Sight Machine deeply parse PLC code. Most competitors don't.
2. **Citation grounding is table stakes** — virtually every new entrant claims source-cited answers. The differentiator is depth of the namespace (UNS, fault history, PLC tags) rather than just PDF Q&A.
3. **remberg is the best-funded European incumbent** — €28M raised, 150+ customers. Not in the US yet, but watch.
4. **The OEM channel is underserved** — Spogen and Industrility are building OEM-facing tools. FactoryLM's "per-plant assessment + namespace" model is orthogonal and could partner here.
5. **Hardware-attached (Augury, Presage) is a different motion** — they own the sensor data. FactoryLM wins on the PLC/manual/fault-history side without requiring new hardware.
6. **The $500 assessment + pilot model is a genuine wedge** — most competitors require a longer sales cycle. Low-friction land is a strategic advantage.
