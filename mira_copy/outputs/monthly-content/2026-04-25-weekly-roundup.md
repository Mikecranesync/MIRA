Sharing a few things we learned this week from working with plant techs:

- That GS20 OC fault? The manual says 'check motor insulation' — but 8/10 times it’s a ground fault in the conduit between VFD and motor. One tech runs a megger on every OC trip. Saved him 3 repeat calls last month.

- Allen-Bradley 1756-L71 MEM2 errors: SD card corruption is more common than firmware mismatches. But don’t pull it while powered — we’ve seen bricked controllers.

- Hydraulic power unit noise after restart? If it’s whining, check the reservoir level *and* the breather cap. Clogged breather = cavitation = premature pump death.

- We trained our AI on real manuals (not generic fixes). When it failed to read hydraulic symbols in photos, we rebuilt the image pipeline. Now it spots a blocked strainer in a photo 9/10 times.
