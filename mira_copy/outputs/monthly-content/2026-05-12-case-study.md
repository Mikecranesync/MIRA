Sharing something that helped our team — might help yours.

We run a mix of Allen-Bradley CompactLogix PLCs and GS20 VFDs. Last year, our avg. time to diagnose a fault code without OEM support was 38 minutes. With manuals missing or outdated, it was worse during shift changes.

We started testing an internal tool — now FactoryLM — that lets us take a photo of any fault screen and get back a diagnosis pulled from our own manuals via AI.

Example: Last week, a GS10 faulted with ‘E01’ — no context on screen. Photo to the app. Back came: ‘Input phase loss detected. Check line-side fuses L1/L2.’

We checked. One fuse blown. Fixed in 15 minutes.

The value wasn’t the answer — it was not waiting.

We built it to stop wasting time hunting pages. It works on phones, no install. If you’re stuck on a fault, you could test it during a break.

No pitch — just sharing what’s working for us.
