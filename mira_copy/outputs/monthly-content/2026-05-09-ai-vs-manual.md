Quick tip for r/PLC: F306 on PowerFlex 525 usually means bus overvoltage during decel.

Fix: Increase decel time or check if load is regenerative (e.g. conveyor downhill). Verify braking resistor connection. If using GS20, check E0-11 and H6-01 settings.
