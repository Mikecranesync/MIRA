Quick tip for GS20 VFDs: F83 (overvoltage during stop) often isn’t supply voltage. 

Check:
- Decel time too short? Default is 10 sec, but long惯性 loads need 25+
- DC bus cap aging? Measure ripple >10% → replace
- Is there regeneration? Add braking resistor if not
