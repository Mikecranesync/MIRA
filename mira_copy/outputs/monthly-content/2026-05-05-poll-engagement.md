Hey gang. Ran into this yesterday and wanted to share — might save someone time.

GS20 VFD throwing F8 (Phase Loss) but input voltage looks solid on meter.

We chased loose lugs for 40 minutes. Turned out: one IGBT module was partially failed, creating high impedance — enough to fake a phase loss under load.

Lesson: before tearing into breakers or feeders, check output waveform with a scope. Even if input looks clean.

Also made me think — how many of you keep PDF manuals accessible on mobile? Or rely on printed binders?
