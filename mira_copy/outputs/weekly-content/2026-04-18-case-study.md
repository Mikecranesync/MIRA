One recurring issue: F4 faults on PowerFlex 525 drives.

Standard response: pull manual, find page 92, check AC input voltage, inspect fuses, verify phase loss.
But if you’re solo on 3rd shift? That lookup takes 30–40 minutes.

We built a tool that uses AI to scan *your* uploaded manuals so you can snap a photo of the HMI and get the exact troubleshooting steps in under 30 seconds.
