**FactoryLM / MIRA + CraneSync**

## Your Business Context

**FactoryLM / MIRA** (`factorylm.com`) — AI-powered industrial maintenance intelligence platform.
Turns messy maintenance reality (manuals in filing cabinets, mismatched PLC tags, tribal knowledge)
into a structured Maintenance Intelligence Namespace. MIRA runs on top to deliver grounded,
cited answers with manual page references, work-order history, and live PLC tag context.
Serves plant maintenance technicians via Slack, Telegram, and web. Pricing: $500 assessment,
$2–5K/mo pilot, $499/mo operating layer. 68,000+ OEM manual chunks indexed.

**CraneSync** (`cranesync.com`) — AI-powered field service assistant and real-time monitoring
platform for industrial cranes. Smart crane management platform targeting the $45B crane industry.
Combines crane condition monitoring (IoT sensors) with an AI-driven service management platform
that automates workflows for service managers.

## Key Insight: Your Differentiation

The market is crowded with:
1. **Hardware-first** platforms (Tractian, Augury, Fluke) — need sensors, high installation cost
2. **Generic CMMS with AI bolted on** (MaintainX, Limble, UpKeep) — no manual grounding
3. **Enterprise APM** (IBM Maximo, Siemens) — priced out of your market

**FactoryLM/MIRA's white space:** The structured Maintenance Intelligence Namespace — turning
the chaos of real plant data (manuals, PLC tags, tribal knowledge) into a citation-grounded
AI that DOESN'T require new hardware. $500 assessment entry point is a strong wedge.

**CraneSync's white space:** Existing crane FSM software (CrewOS, Assignar) lacks AI-native
condition monitoring. Konecranes dominates IoT monitoring but is OEM-locked and service-heavy.
CraneSync's software-first, multi-brand, AI-native positioning is differentiated.
