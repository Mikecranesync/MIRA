## About Your Businesses

### FactoryLM / MIRA (factorylm.com)
AI-powered industrial maintenance diagnostic platform. Turns messy maintenance reality — manuals in filing cabinets, mismatched PLC tags, tribal knowledge — into a structured **Maintenance Intelligence Namespace**. MIRA answers technicians in Slack/Telegram/web with **cited answers** from their own manuals, fault history, and PLC tags.

**Three offerings:** $500 assessment → $2–5K/mo pilot → $499/mo operating layer.  
**Differentiation:** Citation-grounded troubleshooting, UNS structure, no CMMS replacement needed.  
**68,000+ OEM documentation chunks indexed** (Allen-Bradley, Siemens, ABB, Schneider, Yaskawa, Mitsubishi, Rockwell, Honeywell).

### CraneSync (cranesync.com)
AI-powered field service assistant and real-time monitoring platform for industrial equipment maintenance, **starting with overhead cranes**. Two components: (1) crane condition monitoring, (2) AI-driven service management platform automating workflows for service managers. Targets the $45B crane industry.

## Key Observations for FactoryLM / CraneSync

1. **The direct threat is MaintainX + Limble** — well-funded, mobile-first CMMS platforms building AI layers. FactoryLM's moat is the *structured knowledge namespace* they can't easily replicate.

2. **Tractian and Augury converge from the sensor side** — they're adding knowledge and troubleshooting layers to their sensor stacks. This is the long-term collision course, but both are expensive ($50K+/year enterprise deals).

3. **Fracttal's January 2026 funding round** means fresh European expansion — watch for US push.

4. **Fiix (Rockwell) is a channel threat** — Rockwell's existing relationships with Allen-Bradley customers put Fiix directly in front of FactoryLM's ICP buyers.

5. **Factory AI (f7i.ai) is the closest startup analog** — similar brownfield positioning, similar AI copilot vision, but they require sensors. FactoryLM's document-first approach has lower friction.

6. **CraneSync's nearest competitor is crewOS** — same overhead crane vertical, similar field service management focus. Key differentiator: CraneSync's AI condition monitoring vs. crewOS's field scheduling focus.

7. **Bosch acquiring Uptake (May 2026)** signals that industrial AI is now platform-consolidation phase — expect more acquisitions.
