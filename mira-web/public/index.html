PowerFlex 753 on Line 3 tripped on fault F-012 twice in the last 15 min — both right after startup. Root cause? And which params should I check?

M
MIRA · answered in 1.8 s

F-012 on a PowerFlex 753 = motor overload trip. The drive hit its thermal I²t limit based on F501 Motor OL Current × F502 Motor OL Hertz.

Two trips inside 15 min of startup narrows it to three causes, ranked by likelihood:

  1. F501 set too low vs. motor nameplate FLA — most common miscommissioning
  2. Blocked airflow / clogged motor fan
  3. Mechanical bind on the driven load

Check F501 against the nameplate FLA first — it's the fastest test.
