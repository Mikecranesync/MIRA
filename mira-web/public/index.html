Fault E-731 on the FANUC R-30iB, axis 2 following error
Axis 2 following error E-731 typically indicates encoder feedback loss or excessive position error. Check the J2 encoder cable at CN2 connector — look for pinch damage near the J2 axis housing.

If cable is intact, verify pulse coder battery voltage (should be >3.1V).
[§12.4 FANUC R-30iB Maintenance p.284]
