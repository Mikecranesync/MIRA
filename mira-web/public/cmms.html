How It Works

1. Describe the Fault

Type a fault code, describe a symptom, or snap a photo of the display. Mira searches your actual equipment manuals — Allen-Bradley, PowerFlex, Automation Direct, hydraulic systems — and returns the diagnostic steps in seconds, even at 2am.

2. Get the Fix, Not a Guess

Mira tells you why it's failing, not just when. It cites the exact manual page and section — like consulting an experienced colleague who has already read every manual on the floor. No generic internet answers. No guesswork.

3. Your CMMS Stays in Charge

Mira works alongside MaintainX, Limble, or UpKeep. On the $297/month plan, it writes the work order directly into your existing system — fault description, probable cause, and recommended fix. No double-entry.

Reduce Unplanned Downtime — Without Replacing What Already Works

Your CMMS tracks work orders. Mira catches what your most experienced tech would catch — the fault your team has seen before but can't quite remember at midnight. It works where your team already is: Telegram, Slack, or a browser on their phone. No training. No app install. No IT ticket.

The average maintenance technician wastes 20–40 minutes per fault flipping through PDFs. Mira finds the answer from the same manual in seconds. That's the difference between a 30-minute fix and a 2-hour shutdown — on equipment you already own.
