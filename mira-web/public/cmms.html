How It Works

1. Ask Mira Your Fault Code

Type your fault code, describe a symptom, or snap a photo. Mira searches your actual equipment manuals — Allen-Bradley, PowerFlex, Automation Direct, hydraulic systems — and returns the diagnostic steps in seconds.

2. Get the Answer, Not a Guess

Mira cites the exact manual page and section. No generic internet answers. No hallucinated procedures. Your OEM documentation, searchable by AI, on the floor where you need it.

3. Work Order Created Automatically

When Mira diagnoses a fault, it auto-generates a work order in your CMMS with the fault description, probable cause, and recommended fix. No double-entry. No clipboard.
