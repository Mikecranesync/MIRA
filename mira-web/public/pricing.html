Questions

What's included in the free tier?
10 diagnostic queries per month, manual uploads, and cited answers from your OEM documentation. No credit card required. Enough to see if MIRA is useful for your shop.
What does "site license" mean?
One price covers your entire team — 5 technicians or 50. No per-user fees, no seat counting. Add people whenever you need to.
Does MIRA replace my CMMS?
No. Your CMMS is where work orders live. MIRA is where the troubleshooting happens. On the $297/mo plan, MIRA writes work orders directly into MaintainX, Limble, or UpKeep — your techs never leave the tools they know.
Why bundle all three CMMS adapters?
Because per-adapter pricing feels like nickel-and-diming. One price, all three adapters. If you switch CMMSes, MIRA still works.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly billing, cancel anytime from your billing portal. No contracts, no annual commitments.
What if I need help getting set up?
All plans include email support. The Integrated plan ($297/mo) includes priority support with faster response times.
