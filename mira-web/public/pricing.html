Frequently asked

Quick answers to common questions

What counts as a "facility"?
A facility is a single physical site — one plant, one warehouse, one building. Multiple buildings at the same address count as one facility. Separate physical addresses are separate facilities. Community covers one facility; Professional covers up to three.
How does the 7-day free trial work?
Sign up with email — no credit card required. You get full Professional-tier access for 7 days. At the end of the trial you choose a plan or your account enters a read-only mode. Nothing is deleted.
What is the Knowledge Cooperative?
The Knowledge Cooperative is our shared OEM manual database. When you upload a VFD or compressor manual, MIRA extracts diagnostic knowledge and adds it to a pool shared across all Community and Professional customers — anonymized and aggregated. The more customers contribute, the smarter MIRA gets for everyone. This is FactoryLM's core network-effect moat.
Can I switch plans mid-month?
Yes. Upgrades are prorated immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. Annual plan credits are applied to the new plan price.
Is annual billing paid upfront?
Yes — annual plans are billed once per year at the discounted rate (20% off monthly pricing). You can pay by card or ACH. Enterprise customers can pay by invoice.
Does FactoryLM connect to my existing CMMS?
FactoryLM includes Atlas CMMS built-in. It also syncs with Fiix, MaintainX, and UpKeep via API on Professional and Enterprise plans. Full ERP connectors (SAP PM, Oracle) are on the Enterprise roadmap.
