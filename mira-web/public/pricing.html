Questions

Clear boundaries before you buy.

Do I need the $500 assessment first?

No. It is optional. Start with PrintSense or Drive Commander directly. The assessment is for plants asking for a broader rollout plan.

Is PrintSense only a managed pilot?

No. PrintSense is the standalone product: send prints and chat with them. Complete-package workspaces and reviewed assurance are rollout options inside the same product, not a separate consulting identity.

Does PrintSense replace engineering review?

No. It is read-only explanation and troubleshooting intelligence. It cites evidence, exposes uncertainty, and escalates safety-critical or ambiguous cases.

What does Drive Commander cover?

Supported drive families with graded evidence packs. Public pages show cited fault information; Pro adds the deeper troubleshooting and workflow guidance available for that pack.

What is MIRA?

MIRA is the team knowledge and execution layer behind the standalone products. It becomes useful when you want print and drive knowledge connected to plant assets, manuals, history, telemetry, and work systems.
