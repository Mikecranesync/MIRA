# Broader Magnetek (CMCO) opportunity map

Discovery inventory of Magnetek material-handling products ADJACENT to the crane
drives — what may later fit FactoryLM/MIRA, and **where each belongs**. The point
is to classify, NOT to force everything into the Drive Commander drive-pack schema.

## Classification summary

| Belongs in | Products |
|---|---|
| **drive_commander** (fits current VFD drive-pack schema) | IMPULSE G+/VG+ Series 5, Series 4, **G+ Mini**, Sway Control, Dynamic Braking Units & Resistors |
| **future_crane_commander_pack** (operator control + brakes + collision) | Flex EX2/EX/Fit/Wave/Duo radios, SLTX/MLTX2 bellybox, MHR receivers, SBN/SBP2 pendants, 200S/300M/400D brakes, Braketronic + brake rectifier, LaserGuard 2 / Mini |
| **controls_pack** (panels, automation orchestration, power infrastructure) | AC engineered/pre-engineered panels, Intelli-Protect, Intelli-Guide auto-dispatch, ELECTROBAR conductor bars, festoon/cable reels |
| **machine_pack** (mechanical/sensing) | Black Max / Blue Max hoist motors, motor encoders |
| **maintenance_diagnostic** | Intelli-Connect crane monitoring |
| **not_a_fit** (different domain) | DC crane drives, Quattro elevator drives, M-Force mining drives |

## Why this matters for the pack architecture

- **Drive Commander stays a *drive* pack.** Only the AC crane VFDs (+ the drive-embedded
  sway/dynamic-braking features) belong in the current schema. Everything else needs a
  *different* pack shape.
- **A future "Crane Commander" pack** is the natural home for the operator/brake/collision
  layer (radios, pendants, brakes, LaserGuard) — these are crane-safety-critical but are NOT
  VFD parameter/fault decode. Attempting to cram a brake controller into a drive pack would
  pollute the schema.
- **Intelli-Connect** is the interesting maintenance-diagnostic adjacency — it already does
  VFD monitoring/trending, i.e. the same signal-difference story MIRA tells. A later
  integration surface, not a drive pack.

## Recommendation

Keep Drive Commander scoped to the AC crane VFD families (G+ Mini → Series 4 → Series 5).
Open a separate discovery track for a **Crane Commander** pack (radios/pendants/brakes/
collision) and a **controls** pack (panels/automation) rather than expanding the drive schema.
